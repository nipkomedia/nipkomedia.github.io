Nezdor FAQ

Nezdor is designed to be playable without hand-holding. This page covers the “okay but how do I…?” stuff.
How do I save my game?

Use the in-game save option. Click the Inventory (treasure chest) icon. Then click "Save Game". "Exit Game" will also save the game and take you back to the Main menu.

I looted items — where do they go?

Looted items go into your inventory (treasure chest icon). If your inventory is full, looted items will fall to the ground in a loot bag. After you free space (delete, use, or store items at a bank), you can retrieve the loot bag contents.

How do I equip weapons and armor?

Open your Inventory (treasure chest icon). Select a weapon or armor item, then drag it to the appropriate equipment slot to equip it.

How does crafting work?

Crafting requires a recipe or pattern, materials, and sometimes a tool. Buy recipes, patterns, or tools from merchants, then gather the required materials. Use the correct crafting station (Workbench, Oven, Alchemist Lab). If you are successful, you will no longer need to repurchase the recipe or pattern, but you must still supply the tools and materials to craft more.

Where can I store items and coins?

Use the Bank. It’s the safe place for overflow inventory and currency management.

I finished a quest. Is there more?

Most quests have follow-up quests. Read the final statement from the quest giver for clues about where to go next in the quest chain. If you miss the dialog, check your Conversation Log — it records the final message upon quest completion.

How do I report a bug or send feedback?

Email NipkoMedia@gmail.com with what happened, where you were, and what you expected.
